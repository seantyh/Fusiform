## Affective Lexicon (From Lu, 2015)

Manual annotated 10,032 lemmas on five emotion categories (Ekman's basic emotion):
Happy(1), Sad(2), Fear(3), Anger(4), Surprise(5)

Frequency distribution is as follows:
